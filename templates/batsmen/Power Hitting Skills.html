Power Hitting Skills

Goals: High number of 6s and high strike rate.
Training Modules:
   Boundary Hitting Drills:Practice hitting 6s against both spin and pace in the nets.Focus on power-hitting techniques, like body position and swing mechanics.
   Strength and Conditioning:Weight training and core exercises to build strength and power.Plyometric exercises to enhance explosive power.
   Range Hitting:Use a range with targets set at different distances to practice hitting consistently long shots.Work on hitting with controlled power to avoid unnecessary risks.
