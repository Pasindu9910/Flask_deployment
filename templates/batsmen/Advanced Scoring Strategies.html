Advanced Scoring Strategies

Goals: High average, high strike rate, frequent 50s and 100s.
Training Modules:
   Shot Selection Drills:Practice choosing the right shots for different deliveries in the nets,Focus on minimizing risk while maximizing runs, emphasizing placement over power.
   Situational Batting:Set up different match scenarios and practice building innings, such as starting slow and accelerating, or managing high-pressure chases.
   Rotation of Strike:Drills that focus on rotating the strike, such as hitting to specific fielding gaps, or practicing quick singles and doubles.
   Power Hitting and Placement:Combine boundary-hitting practice with precise placement to find gaps.Use range-hitting exercises to practice hitting 4s and 6s from different types of deliveries.
