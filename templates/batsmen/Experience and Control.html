Experience and Control

Goals: High number of runs and balls faced, high average.
Training Modules:
   Long Innings Practice:Simulate long innings in the nets, focusing on concentration and endurance. Practice maintaining focus over extended periods and adjusting to different bowlers.
   Reading the Game:Scenario-based drills to improve decision-making and adaptability. Practice adjusting batting strategy based on match situations, bowlers, and pitch conditions.
   Game Simulation:Participate in match simulations to practice building innings and pacing an innings. Focus on rotating the strike, playing with soft hands, and maintaining a high strike rate.
