Consistent Batting Techniques

Goals: High average, moderate to high number of 4s, low strike rate.
Training Modules:
   Technical Drills:Focus on refining batting technique, including footwork, balance, and shot execution. Use throwdowns and bowling machines to practice against specific types of deliveries.
   Playing Along the Ground:Practice playing along the ground to avoid risky aerial shots. Drills to enhance timing and placement, focusing on hitting gaps for 4s.
   Patience and Shot Selection:Simulated long batting sessions to develop patience and shot selection. Emphasize leaving balls outside off-stump and focusing on scoring opportunities.
