Fundamentals and Basics

Goals: Low number of 6s, moderate strike rate, and moderate to high number of 4s.
Training Modules:
   Basic Technique Drills:Practice the fundamentals of batting, including stance, grip, and footwork. Regular throwdowns and net sessions focusing on basic shot execution.
   Running Between Wickets:Drills to improve running between wickets, focusing on quick singles and converting ones into twos. Practice calling and coordination with the batting partner.
   Field Placement Awareness:Practice placing shots into gaps and understanding field placements. Drills to enhance awareness of fielders and finding gaps for boundaries.
